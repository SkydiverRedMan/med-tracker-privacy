Privacy Policy – Med Tracker

Med Tracker collects information provided by users when they log medication usage through the skill.

Information Collected

  • Medication log entries (date/time and medication name)
  • An anonymous Alexa user identifier

Use of Information

Information is used solely to provide the skill’s functionality.

Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties.

Data Retention

Data is retained until the user disables the skill.
